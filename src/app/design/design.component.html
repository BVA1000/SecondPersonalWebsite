Project Design

One of my most favorite things about programming is the design element. I love the design part of every coding project that I have done because this is where I get to experiment and be creative. As my father always told me "Great Presentation is the key to Success!", and this is very true. I am still learning more, but designing a project from the color of the background to the style of the buttons, makes me feel like I am an artist. To me building an application is like painting a great work of art. Both take time and effort.
