My Career Goals

What that will look like for me is still uncertain, but I do know that I want to work for myself, but it will take some time and skills to develop and also finding the right people to start something great. This is why for the time being I want to work for a company that recruits fresh developers like myself who have coding bootcamp knowledge but need real life work experience. I would not mind working for either a well known establishment or a new start-up, but would like to be in a productive environment with great values and has a mindset for success. I like working with smart and responsible people and with the right team anything is possible.

I have also had the thought of freelancing. The way I would go about this is I would have work in a tech company, and after I have mastered my craft I would use my free time to create side projects for clients who are looking for developers like to create applications for either their personal sites or for their business. I like the idea of freelancing because it encourages me to have an entrepreneurial mindset, which is valuable in the long run. I also think that it would be cool to build and create functional websites and applications for people who are willing to pay me for my work and would give me more experience in creating different types of projects.

This is the digital are and I believe as developers it is crusial for us know how to build new technologies and algorhythms that would build protection and stability to our digital world.
